Inspired by a famous South Asian kite-flying festival "Basant". 
Bo Kata, is a high-speed aerial battle royale game between kites 
where players use power-ups, employ various combat techniques 
like string cuts and wraps to defeat their opponents. This game 
includes challenges like tight spaces and strong winds. Players 
must navigate these constraints while racing to keep their kite 
intact through various defense and offense techniques to become 
the ultimate champion.
